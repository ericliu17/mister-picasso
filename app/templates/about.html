Hello! I am Mister Picasso.

I am here to paint your portrait in my signature style.



To generate your painting, I begin by using a VGG-16 pre-trained convolutional neural network.



Using the high-level features of your image and the style image derived from this model, I calculate pixel-wise and patch-wise feature correlations between your image and the style image. These allow me to capture features like colors, shapes, and textures.



Pixel-wise and patch-wise feature correlations are captured by gram matrices and 2D spatial convolutions, respectively.
